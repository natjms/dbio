dbio

Instantly publish your profile to IPFS. No server required.

What is dbio?

dbio aims to recreate the simplicity of about.me using IPFS. Simply fill out the form, hit publish, and dbio will create and store a JSON file representing your profile on your IPFS node. You can then view your profile through the website, accessing it from its hash.

To use this app, your node needs to be writable and have CORS enabled. See here for instructions on how to prepare your node.
